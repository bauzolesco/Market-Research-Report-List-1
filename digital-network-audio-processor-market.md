<p><p>The Digital Network Audio Processor market features various standards, primarily including Dante and Livewire. The Dante standard facilitates high-quality, low-latency audio over Ethernet networks, making it ideal for live sound and broadcast environments. Conversely, Livewire, developed for broadcasting, emphasizes synchronized audio and control data transmission across a network. Both standards enhance audio distribution and flexibility, catering to diverse applications such as live performances, studio environments, and radio broadcasting, enabling seamless integration and operational efficiency.</p></p>
