<p><p>DC-DC converters are essential for electric vehicles (EVs) as they efficiently manage voltage levels between the battery and various components. In the isolated market, converters use transformers to separate the input and output circuits, enhancing safety and flexibility, ideal for high-voltage applications. Non-isolated converters, on the other hand, directly link input to output, offering high efficiency and compact design for lower voltage applications. Both types play crucial roles in optimizing EV performance and extending range.</p></p>
