<p><p>The bus tachograph market is divided into two main types: integrated and portable systems. Integrated tachographs are built directly into the bus's existing electronics, providing seamless data collection and advanced features like real-time tracking and comprehensive reporting. In contrast, portable tachographs are standalone devices that can be easily moved between vehicles, offering flexibility for smaller fleets or for use in various settings. Both types enable compliance with regulations governing driving hours and vehicle usage, enhancing operational efficiency.</p></p>
