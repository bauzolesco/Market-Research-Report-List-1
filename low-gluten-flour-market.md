<p><ul><li>Machine Milled Flour</li><li>Stone Ground Flour</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The low-gluten flour market is primarily categorized into machine milled flour and stone ground flour. Machine milled flour is produced through industrial processes that ensure uniform particle size and consistency, making it suitable for mass production. Conversely, stone ground flour is crafted using traditional methods, involving stones that preserve the grain's natural nutrition and flavor, appealing to health-conscious consumers. Both types cater to different culinary needs, with machine milled flour favored for baking and stone ground flour preferred for artisanal products.</p></p>
