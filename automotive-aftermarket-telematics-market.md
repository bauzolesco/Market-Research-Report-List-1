<p><strong>The Automotive Aftermarket Telematics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Plug and Play Telematics</li><li>Hardwired Install Telematics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive aftermarket telematics market offers two primary types: plug and play telematics and hardwired install telematics. Plug and play telematics devices are user-friendly and easily installed by plugging them into the vehicle's OBD-II port, allowing for quick access to data like diagnostics and location tracking. In contrast, hardwired install telematics require professional installation, directly integrating into the vehicle's electrical system for more comprehensive data collection and advanced features, making them suitable for fleet management and specialized applications.</p></p>
