<p><ul><li>Continuous Wave (CW) Fiber Laser</li><li>Pulsed Fiber Laser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The High Power Fiber Laser market is primarily categorized into Continuous Wave (CW) Fiber Lasers and Pulsed Fiber Lasers. CW Fiber Lasers emit a constant beam of light, ideal for applications requiring steady energy delivery, such as cutting and welding. In contrast, Pulsed Fiber Lasers produce light in short bursts, offering high peak power for tasks like marking and fine engraving. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs, enhancing precision and efficiency in manufacturing processes.</p></p>
