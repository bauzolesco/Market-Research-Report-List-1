<p><p>An Autothermal Reformer (ATR) is a technology that converts hydrocarbon feedstocks into synthesis gas (syngas) through a combination of partial oxidation and steam methane reforming. The ATR market can be categorized into two types: non-desulfurization and desulfurization. Non-desulfurization ATR processes handle feedstocks with lower sulfur content, while desulfurization ATR processes are designed to remove sulfur compounds, allowing for the use of higher sulfur feeds. This distinction is crucial for applications in cleaner energy production and petrochemical synthesis.</p></p>
