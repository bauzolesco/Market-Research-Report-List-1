<p><strong>The ETC Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>RSU</li><li>OBU</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ETC (Electronic Toll Collection) equipment market features two primary types: Roadside Units (RSUs) and On-Board Units (OBUs). RSUs are installed on roadside infrastructure to communicate with vehicles, facilitating toll collection and traffic management. OBUs are mounted inside vehicles, allowing them to receive signals from RSUs and process toll payments automatically. Together, these systems enable seamless toll transactions, enhancing traffic flow and reducing congestion on highways, thus improving overall transportation efficiency.</p></p>
