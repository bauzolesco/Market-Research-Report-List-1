<p><p>Thermoforming packaging technology encompasses three primary market types: vacuum packaging, pressure packaging, and mechanical packaging. Vacuum packaging reduces air around the product, enhancing shelf life and freshness by minimizing oxidation. Pressure packaging utilizes elevated atmospheric pressures to maintain product integrity and extend durability, often used for fragile items. Mechanical packaging employs physical methods, such as sealing and folding materials, to create protective barriers, ideal for diverse applications. Collectively, these methods optimize product protection, enhance user convenience, and improve supply chain efficiency.</p></p>
