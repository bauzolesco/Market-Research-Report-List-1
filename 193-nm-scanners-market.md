<p><p>The 193 nm scanners market is primarily divided into two types: dry scanners and wet scanners. Dry scanners utilize a photoresist coating that is processed in a dry environment, offering faster throughput and lower operational costs. Conversely, wet scanners employ a liquid immersion technique to enhance resolution and depth of focus, allowing for smaller and more intricate chip designs. Both types serve the semiconductor manufacturing industry, but they cater to different application needs and production environments.</p></p>
