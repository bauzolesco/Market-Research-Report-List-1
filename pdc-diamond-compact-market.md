<p><ul><li>HPHT</li><li>CVD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PDC (Polycrystalline Diamond Compact) market primarily consists of two types: HPHT (High-Pressure High-Temperature) and CVD (Chemical Vapor Deposition). HPHT diamonds are created by mimicking the natural processes that form diamonds deep within the Earth, resulting in durable and high-performance cutting tools often used in industrial applications. In contrast, CVD diamonds are synthesized through gas-phase chemical reactions, allowing for precise control over properties. Both methods cater to specific needs in drilling, mining, and manufacturing sectors, driving diverse market demands.</p></p>
