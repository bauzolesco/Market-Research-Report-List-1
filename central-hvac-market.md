<p><strong>The Central HVAC Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Split System Central HVAC</li><li>Packaged Unit Central HVAC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Central HVAC market features two main types: Split System Central HVAC and Packaged Unit Central HVAC. Split System Central HVAC consists of separate indoor and outdoor units, allowing for efficient temperature control and flexibility in installation. In contrast, Packaged Unit Central HVAC combines all components—compressor, condenser, and evaporator—into a single outdoor unit, simplifying installation and maintenance. Both systems cater to varying space, budget, and efficiency requirements, making them suitable for residential and commercial applications.</p></p>
