<p><p>The automotive particulate matter sensor market is categorized by different particle sizes detected: PM1.0, PM2.5, and PM10, along with other particulate matter types. PM1.0 sensors measure particles with diameters of 1 micron or smaller, crucial for assessing fine particulate pollution. PM2.5 sensors detect particles up to 2.5 microns, linked to respiratory issues. PM10 sensors cover particles up to 10 microns, which can include dust and pollen. The "Other" category may encompass specific sensors addressing unique particulate sizes or applications in vehicles.</p></p>
