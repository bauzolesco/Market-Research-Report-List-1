<p><ul><li>Single Layer</li><li>Multi-layer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Continuous form paper is utilized in various market types, primarily categorized into single-layer and multi-layer formats. Single-layer continuous form paper consists of a single sheet, suitable for standard printing tasks and invoices. In contrast, multi-layer continuous form paper comprises multiple layers, allowing the creation of carbon copies or multiple prints simultaneously. This type is often used for forms, receipts, and shipping documents, providing efficiency in data duplication while streamlining the printing process for businesses that require multiple copies of a document.</p></p>
