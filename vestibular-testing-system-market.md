<p><p>The vestibular testing system market encompasses various types, including Videonystagmography (VNG) and Electronystagmography (ENG), among others. VNG utilizes video recording technology to evaluate eye movements and balance, providing a detailed assessment of vestibular function. ENG, on the other hand, measures electrical activity of the eye muscles to detect nystagmus and other disorders. Other market types may include rotary chair tests and posturography, which offer additional diagnostic insights into vestibular system functioning and balance disorders.</p></p>
