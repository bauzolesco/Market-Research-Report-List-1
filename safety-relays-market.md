<p><ul><li>Electromechanical Relay</li><li>Solid State Relay</li><li>Thermal Relay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The safety relays market comprises three main types: Electromechanical Relays, Solid State Relays, and Thermal Relays. Electromechanical Relays use physical moving parts to create a switch, providing reliable isolation but with slower response times. Solid State Relays utilize semiconductor technology for faster switching speeds and increased durability, lacking moving parts. Thermal Relays protect motors from overload by sensing temperature changes. Each type serves specific applications, balancing speed, reliability, and protection in safety-critical environments.</p></p>
