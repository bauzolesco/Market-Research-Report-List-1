<p><p>The High Performance Automotive Elastomers market comprises two primary types: Thermoplastic Elastomers (TPE) and Thermosetting Elastomers (TSE). TPEs combine the advantages of rubber and plastics, offering flexibility, durability, and recyclability, making them ideal for various automotive applications like seals and gaskets. TSEs, on the other hand, are heat-cured, providing excellent thermal stability, chemical resistance, and longevity, suitable for components exposed to extreme conditions. Both types enhance vehicle performance, safety, and sustainability in the automotive sector.</p></p>
