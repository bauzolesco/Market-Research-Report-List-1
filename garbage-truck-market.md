<p><p>The garbage truck market is segmented into three main types: front loaders, rear loaders, and side loaders. Front loaders feature large, open containers for quick loading from the front, ideal for commercial waste management. Rear loaders have their collection mechanisms at the back, allowing for a straightforward design suited for residential pickups. Side loaders use automated arms to collect bins placed on the curbside, enhancing efficiency and safety. Each type caters to different waste collection needs and operational environments.</p></p>
