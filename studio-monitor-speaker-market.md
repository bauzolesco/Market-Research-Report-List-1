<p><p>The studio monitor speaker market is primarily divided into two types: active and passive speakers. Active speakers incorporate built-in amplifiers, allowing for easier setup and power management, ideal for compact studio environments. They typically offer better audio clarity and require less external equipment. Passive speakers, on the other hand, rely on external amplifiers, offering more flexibility in system design but needing additional components. Both types cater to various user preferences and acoustic needs in professional audio production and home studios.</p></p>
