<p><strong>The Mark Pen Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Aqueous Mark Pen</li><li>Oily Mark Pen</li><li>Alcohol Mark Pen</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mark pens are categorized into three main types based on their solvent composition: aqueous, oily, and alcohol-based. Aqueous mark pens use water as their primary solvent, making them ideal for non-toxic applications and easy clean-up. Oily mark pens, with an oil-based solvent, offer superior permanence and smudge resistance, suited for various surfaces. Alcohol mark pens utilize alcohol as a solvent, providing quick-drying properties and vibrant colors, commonly used for art and illustration. Each type serves distinct purposes in creative and practical applications.</p></p>
