<p><p>The nitrogen testing market comprises two primary types: instrument testing and chemical testing. Instrument testing involves the use of advanced analytical devices such as mass spectrometers and gas chromatographs to measure nitrogen content accurately in various samples. In contrast, chemical testing employs traditional methods, including colorimetric assays and titrations, to determine nitrogen levels. Both approaches serve critical roles in fields like agriculture, environmental monitoring, and food safety, providing essential data for optimizing processes and ensuring compliance with standards.</p></p>
