<p><p>Positron Emission Tomography (PET) systems are categorized into three main market types. </p><p>Direct Imaging utilizes digital detectors to directly capture photon signals emitted by positron annihilation, providing high-resolution images. </p><p>Indirect Imaging involves converting emitted photons into visible light, which is then detected and analyzed, typically resulting in lower resolution compared to direct methods. </p><p>Alternative Imaging encompasses innovative techniques that may utilize hybrid systems, combining PET with other imaging modalities like CT or MRI to enhance diagnostic capabilities and provide comprehensive imaging solutions.</p></p>
